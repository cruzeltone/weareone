Welcome to We Are One

Our mission is to raise awareness and break the stigma surrounding mental health. Join us as we empower individuals and create a compassionate community where mental well-being is prioritized.

About the Founder

Cruzz Eltone is a passionate advocate for mental health awareness. As the founder of We Are One, Cruzz started the organization with a simple but powerful vision: to end the stigma surrounding mental health and provide resources for those who are struggling. His mission is to foster a society where mental health is treated with the same importance as physical health.

Our Mission

We Are One is committed to educating the public, supporting mental health initiatives, and creating safe spaces for individuals to seek help without judgment. Our focus is on raising awareness, sharing resources, and advocating for mental health policies that ensure everyone can access the care they need.
