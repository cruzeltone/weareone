About Us

At "We Are One," we provide compassionate mental health support and care, dedicated to empowering individuals through their mental wellness journeys. Our goal is to foster a safe, understanding environment for everyone seeking help.

Founder: Cruzz Eltone, the visionary behind "We Are One," believes in breaking the stigma around mental health and offering holistic and personalized care for mental well-being.His dream of starting such an organisation also started with untold scars he never shared to anyone. Despite having the desire to start such an organization,he didn't launch it till later in June,2024 where he looked for people with untold stories in the growing platform 'TikTok' then later advanced to creating a WhatsApp group.

Our Services

Therapy & Counseling

Individual and group therapy sessions aimed at providing support and strategies for managing mental health challenges.

Emergency Mental Health Support

Immediate assistance and crisis intervention services for individuals experiencing mental health emergencies.

Specialist Consultations

Consultations with mental health specialists, including psychiatrists, psychologists, and counselors, for specialized care.
